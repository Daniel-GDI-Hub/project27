Scribbles on the scribbles

The blog of Project27 is split into two sections: TWIST and the Project log. Both sections are themed around science, accessibility, and life style, but from slightly different angles.

Project log of Project27

Project log is a set of scribbles on topics that I found worth mentioning in context of developing Project27. These topics mainly focus on working with MathML, LaTeX, and web content authoring tools, such as markdown. A cross platform evaluation of mathematical content accessibility is a key objective of the project log.
