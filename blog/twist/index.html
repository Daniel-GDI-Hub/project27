What is twisted in here?

To start with, I TWIST and turn words and scribbles in four topics. Let's just call these "TWIST originals". But I also have two older topics archived in here

Accessibility adventures

There are so many things in life that give us the opportunity to think about how different people with different needs approach an adventure. Let it be a bus journey, a long weekend in the woods, or a sailing trip across the Atlantic, I like to speculate on one or another adventure of my life, and write down the way I see it.

Infinit identities

I am not just I. I am Me, myself, and I. I believe it is important to understand that we can not stick labels on people. The way I see a person is as a collection of identities. Through these set of scribbles, I'd like to illustrate this point through my own identities. As my good friend once said - "Daniel, you are 50 shades of Daniel".

Reading ranger

Once you know yourself, yes, all many of yourselves, it is time to get to know others. Personal relationships are fantastic, but physical connection is not always viable. Luckily, the greatest invention of time - writing - enable you and I to meet, and have a conversation with anyone. Well, anyone who decided to write a book and published it. In the scribbles on reading, I'll attempt to paint a picture of the conversations between book authors and me.

Sexy science

Science is sexy. Science is beautiful. It does not have to be the boring subject from school. You do not even need to be born as a scientist. You can learn scientific thinking, and there are many awesome science communication projects helping you with this. I hope to post about some of my favourites in this topic.

Gone Grapheel

Once upon a time... sorry, I mean: A long, long time ago, in a galaxy far away... there was a project called Grapheel. The Grapheel archive does what it says on the tin. Since I authored some of those earlier posts, and I think they are still valuable, I decided to archive them on TWIST. However, no new scribbles will be added here.

Numerous nanotip

Nanotip is a set of scribbles, which are bite size, nanoscopic, or relatively short considering my wordy way of writing. None of the less, I hope these little tips will help the reader. On the first place me, Mr gold fish memory.
