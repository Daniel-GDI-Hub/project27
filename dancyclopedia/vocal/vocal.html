Welcome to Vocal!

What is Vocal?

April 11, 2020 by Daniel

Vocal has a threefold purpose. First, it aims to provide a platform for practicing journalism and interviewing skills.

Second, it gives an opportunity to get hands on with audio recording, editing, and publishing.

Third, and most important, Vocal is a chance to listen to, learn from, and honour family, friends, and other acquaintances. The people who surround us, are the people who shape us. It is vital to remember their voices, their stories. Vocal is a gallery of such personal journeys spoken by anyone I've once met. My wish is that these voices will be available for the generations to come.
