Scribbles of a blind scientist

Dancyclopedia is a guide to do tasks, I once learnt how to do. Sadly, unless practice follows learning, all knowledge fades. The key objective of Dancyclopedia is to mitigate fading of knowledge in unpracticed tasks, by providing a quick guide of specific topics.
