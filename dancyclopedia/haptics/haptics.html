Volume on haptics

Haptics refers to the branch of science, studying human factors of touch, and performing research and development on technology which facilitates tactile interaction.

Haptic technology

Haptic technology refers to the use of technology that stimulates the senses of touch and motion, especially to reproduce in remote operation or computer simulation the sensations that would be felt by a user interacting directly with physical objects.

Human factors of haptics

Human factors of haptics study the effect of touch and proprioception on tactile perception, cognition, emotions, and behaviour.
