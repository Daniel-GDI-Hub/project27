Chapter on classical mechanics

Mechanics is the branch of applied mathematics dealing with motion and forces producing motion. Kinematics and dynamics form part of classical mechanics.

Kinematics

Kinematics is the branch of mechanics concerned with the motion of objects without reference to the forces which cause the motion.

Dynamics

Dynamics is the branch of mechanics concerned with the motion of bodies under the action of forces.
