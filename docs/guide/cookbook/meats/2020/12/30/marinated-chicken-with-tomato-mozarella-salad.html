Ingredients

Here is what you need to have at home.

Ingredient Quantity Unit
chicken thigh 1 kg
baking bag with marinating seasoning 1 bag
tomatoes 4 pieces
Mozarella cheese 150 g
fresh garlic 4 cloves
olive oil 2 tbsp
balsamic vinegar 2 tbsp
dried (or fresh) basil. handful -

Serves: Make sure to share, and buy for your pals.

Preparation

Here are the steps to cook the meal. Takes as long as it takes. Share and enjoy!

  1. Play music in the kitchen. I recommend this playlist.
  2. Pore a glass of drink for the chef.
  3. Marinate the chicken using the seasoning provided with the baking bag, and let it rest while you prepare the salad.
  4. In a dish, lay down a layer of sliced tomato. Add the mozarella slices on top.
  5. Now add the basil, garlic, a bit of oil and balsamic vinegar.
  6. Repeat the previous step for an other layer.
  7. While the salad is resting, place the marinated chicken in the baking bag, and seal it. Donâ€™t forget to punch a few holes to let the steam escape.
  8. bake for 45-50 minutes on 180 degrees.
  9. Serve the chicken with the tomato salad.
