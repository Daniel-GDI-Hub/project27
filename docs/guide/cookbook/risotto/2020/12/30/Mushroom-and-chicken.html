Ingredients

Here is what you need to have at home.

Ingredient Quantity Unit
cooking onion 1 piece
garlic 2 cloves
lard 2 tbsp
butter 25 g
risotto rice 500 g
leek 1 piece
mushroom 300 g
chicken breast 2 pieces
white wine 200 ml
vegetable stock made with two stock cubes 1 l
seasoning: salt and black pepper to your taste -
herbs: Green mixed herbs a handful -

Serves: Make sure to share, and buy for your pals.

Preparation

Here are the steps to cook the meal. Takes as long as it takes. Share and enjoy!

  1. Play music in the kitchen. I recommend this playlist.
  2. Pore a glass of drink for the chef.
  3. Place the lard in a sauce pan, and add the finely chopped onions to fry.
  4. add the chopped mushrooms and sliced leek and wait until it goes soft.
  5. now add the chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  6. add the rice, and pore the wine on it. Stir until wine is absorbed.
  7. add the herb mix and gradually add the stock until the rice goes sticky.
  8. At the end grate the garlic on the risotto and add the butter.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
