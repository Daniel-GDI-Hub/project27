The Blog: Be the leading example

The Project27 Blog is split into two branches: LogBook and TWIST. In the LogBook, we record issues and solutions found during the development of the Project27 Guide. TWIST is all about sharing thoughts to engage with and inspire new ideas.

LogBook

The LogBook is a set of technical posts on topics related to developing Project27. We mainly focus on accessible ways of using MathML and LaTeX. Nevertheless, we also log the use of tools, such as markdown and Jekyll.

TWIST

We share "The Way I See Things". We would like to encourage people with sight-loss and learning difficulties. We wish to inspire their families, teachers, and friends. We want to be open, and lead by example.
