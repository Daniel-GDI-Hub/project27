MathML usability

I'm aware that MathML is a standardised markup language. However, what is not standardised is the way different screen readers handle it. I hope to take notes on anything related to MathML usability. One day this might come handy.

Complete list of posts

The following list is a growing collection of posts in the "MathML usability" category.

1. MathML usability - An introduction to the category

MathML seems to be the best way around web accessibility of mathematical content. However, whether we use content or presentation style MathML, whether we use one web browser or another, or whether we use one screen reader software versus another will impact the usability and user experience of mathematical content.
