Who we are?

We are a small but committed family business with great friends and advisors. View who is in our core team, who gives us advice, and who volunteered to give their time.

The core team

Project27 is managed by this awesome core team.

Daniel Hajas - Project27 co-founder

As a co-founder of Project27, my main contribution is setting the vision, the roadmap, and the project culture. However, I also love to get involved with hands-on development, such as hosting the project on GitHub, and writing Jekyll code. Of course, I write, edit, and review content for the Guide and the blog too.
View Daniel's profile

Danielle Garratt - Project27 co-founder

Danielle is taking a lead on our work with learning disability. She is also researching ways to make the project site look more visually appealing, while doing lots of hands-on CSS development.
View Danni's profile

The advisory team

We wouldn't be where we are today, without the invaluable advice and guidance of our advisors.

Robert Cobden - Systems Advisor

Rob has been a Research Engineer since 2016, at the Sussex Computer-Human Interaction Lab, where Daniel met him. Gradually, Rob became one of Daniel's most trusted friends. Rob is a skilful coder, and maker of things. His role is to review our systems and highlight any inconsistencies or weaknesses.
Learn more about Rob
