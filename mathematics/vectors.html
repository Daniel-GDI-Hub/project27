Vectors

Scalar product

Given two vectors $\vec{A}$ and $\vec{B}$, and the angle $\theta$ between the two vectors, the scalor product can be calculated as: \( \vec{A}\cdot \vec{B} = |A||B| \cos \theta \)

Vector product

Given two vectors $\vec{A}$ and $\vec{B}$, and the angle $\theta$ between the two vectors, the vector product can be calculated as: \( \vec{A}\times \vec{B} = |A||B| \sin \theta \)
