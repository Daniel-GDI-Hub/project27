What is {{ site.brand }}?

{{ site.brand }} is a place for learning. The content draws on lived experiences and professional skills of the {{ site.brand }} team. It is developed by a couple - Daniel and Danielle. Daniel is a scientifically-minded blind person, with an interest in social entrepreneurship. Danielle covers art, media, gaming, and self-advocacy, while expertly navigating the world of learning disability.

Our team and services help blind or learning disabled people to develop skills that matter. We help reduce frustrations caused by inaccessible learning tools, and build confidence in people's abilities. We do this through peer support, personalised opportunities for learning, creating and discovering new challenges in a safe place, built by it's community.
