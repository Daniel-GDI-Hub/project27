The {{ site.product }}: A journal of life-long learning

The {{ site.brand }} {{ site.product }} is a set of notes to capture and share life-long learning. Notes are organised into collections of science and lifestyle. The {{ site.product }} is a free resource. Developing the {{ site.product }} is based on projects. Working on these projects is an opportunity for active learning. We capture solutions learnt on the way, and share project outputs in form of notes.

Our aim is to offer a quick reference in new, or unpracticed tasks. In addition, we aim to revise, study, research, innovate, and make aware by publishing notes in the {{ site.product }}.

Science collection

The {{ site.brand }} Science collection is a set of volumes on mathematical and physical sciences, computer literacy, and statistics.
