Capturing stories: Listening Is Inspiration

Capturing stories section is a volume of information on the skills and techniques of audio editing. The art of listening and asking questions and responsible journalism.
But what is a life story? A life story is an account of all the events that happened to someone during their life. Life stories can be given to people for a visual aid and reminder of important events or feelings.
